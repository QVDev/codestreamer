Livecodestream

Broadcast with ease, straight from your browser


What is it?

You can broadcast and record your live coding sessions

Always wanted to start streaming your live coding session?
But too much hassle to set up?
That's why we created Livecodestream.

We got you covered, start straight from your browser.
No need to install any browser extensions or OBS software.
And you don't even need an account. Just start broadcasting without worries.

About

Livecodestream is decentralized

Livecodestream does not rely on huge servers, your stream will be decentralized.
How? By using GUN a "decentralized database for developers"

We created this website out of frustration to setup OBS or install browser extensions for ourself. Now with with all the WebRTC functionality with access to screen sharing, camera and microphone it's easier than ever.

And now we are sharing this with everyone else. Livecodestream does not keep recordings or full videos. Did I mention you can record? Oh yeah, you can stream and record your session. We do not keep these recordings at all. Privacy first. The reference to Pied Piper came during development :)

We are a work in progress, but give it a try...
